# Phase 3 — Early Interview Strategy: Context & Presence

**Goal:** Convert screening into alignment by demonstrating lived thinking, presence, and context.

**Game 1 focus:** Do Not Get Filtered Out. Lead with clarity and warmth — conversation, not audition.

**Identity layering:** Enter through role (clear mandate, standards, outcomes). Express temperament (warmth, enthusiasm) inside the container, after trust is established. Calm about decisions, warm in delivery. Legibility first, personality second.

---

## Step 1: Interviewer Profiling System

Ask: "Who is interviewing you? (Name, title, LinkedIn if available)"

Research the interviewer:
- Education, career path, published content, focus areas
- Likely questions based on their background and role
- Hooks for rapport
- Potential biases to be aware of

Output: **Interviewer Brief** (≤ 1 page)

---

## Step 2: Story Bank (5–7 STAR stories)

Build STAR stories calibrated to:
- The company's diagnosed pain (from Phase 1)
- The interviewer's likely focus areas
- Christos's strongest evidence from career files

Each story: Situation → Task → Action → Result (with metric where possible)

---

## Step 3: First 60-Second Talk Track

Draft the opening sequence:
- **How I got here** (career arc in 1–2 sentences)
- **What I love solving** (connects to this role's pain)
- **Why this now** (specific reason this company is interesting)

---

## Step 4: Clarifying Questions (for recruiter screen)

Draft 3–5 questions that:
- Demonstrate strategic thinking
- Surface unspoken hiring criteria
- Build rapport with the interviewer

---

## Step 5: AI Mock Interview

Offer to run a mock interview session:

```markdown
## Mock Interview

I'll play the interviewer using their likely style and topics.

**Instructions:**
- Answer as you would in the real interview
- I'll ask follow-up questions
- After 3–5 exchanges, I'll give you a Precision + Presence rating and coaching notes

Ready? I'll start with the first question.
```
