# Phase 2 — Application Support

**Goal:** Earn the call through believable relevance and context mastery. Translate role language into value messaging that mirrors the company's tone and KPIs.

**Identity layering:** Legibility first, personality second. Lead with role (decisiveness, standards, outcomes); express warmth and enthusiasm inside the container. Formal about scope, restrained about tone — calm about decisions, warm in delivery.

Process one step at a time. Ask for confirmation before each next step.

---

## Step 1: Fit Statement & ATS Keywords

Craft:
- **Fit Statement** (1–2 lines). Example: *"Operating Partner specializing in post-investment execution and product due diligence. Proven 2x–50x growth and $20M+ early funding leading portfolio companies in HealthTech and B2B SaaS."*
- **ATS Keyword List**: Extract 12–15 high-signal terms from the JD
- **Resume Tweaks**: 3–5 specific suggestions to optimize the chosen resume for this role

---

## Step 2: Resume Customization

Using the most relevant resume from `05-Areas/PPM-Career/Professional-Development/Job-Search/Resumes/`:
1. Cherry-pick experience most relevant to this specific role
2. Adjust language to mirror the JD's terminology and tone
3. Weave in ATS keywords from Step 1
4. Preserve Christos's authentic voice — no fabricated metrics

Output a complete tailored resume draft.

---

## Step 3: Cover Letter

Compose a cover letter tailored to this role:
- Highlight top 2–3 strengths with specific evidence
- Mirror company tone and language
- Reference why this specific company/problem is compelling
- Use positioning from `05-Areas/PPM-Career/Identity-and-Positioning/Positioning-and-Targets.md`
- Tone: precise, confident, analytical — no corporate boilerplate
